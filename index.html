Mengqiao Li is an Ph.D. candidate of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the George Washington University. Her current research focus involves developing novel catalysts in nanoscales, and understanding their transformation and fate in aquatic systems.

Mengqiao is expected to receive her Ph.D. degree from the George Washington University advised by Danmeng Shuai at Environmental Engineering in November 2022. She completed a M.Sc. degree in Chemistry (2018) from Hefei National Laboratory for Physical Sciences at the Miscroscale (HFNL) and a B.S. in Material Physics (2015) from the School of the Gifted Young (SCGY) at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

Mengqiao is a recipient of the C. Ellen Gonter Environmental Chemistry Award (2021), National Scholarship for Graduate Students (China, top 5%, 2017) and Excellent New Student award (2011).
